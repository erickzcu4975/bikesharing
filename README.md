# Bikesharing


## Overview of the Analysis:

 NY Citi Bike data/ August 2019, enabling  data visualization in Tableau to demonstrate the convenience of bike sharing business in Des Moines.


### Purpose:

Analyze thru visualizations and offer  to investors a sight of the successful NYC Citi bike program. Visualization demonstrate:

1. Timing bikes are checked out for all riders / genders, 
2. Show the number of bike trips during the day/week
3. Show a differentiate number of trips per each type of user/gender for each day of the week.


## Resources

**Data Source:** 201908-citibike-tripdata.CSV.

**Software:** Jupyter, Pandas, Tableau, Git_Hub.

## Summary:

The data shows activity of the bike sharing service in August/2019.
Manhattan Island concentrate the majority of rides, execute mainly for male riders in morning and evening rush hours. Bikes are being used as alternative to the metro/bus network.
